GOA, Maharashtra

Located on the southwestern coast of India, Goa is one of the top tourist destinations in the country. This erstwhile Portuguese colony is best known for its serene beaches, forts and churches that date back to the colonial era, wildlife sanctuaries, museums, and rich culinary legacy.
